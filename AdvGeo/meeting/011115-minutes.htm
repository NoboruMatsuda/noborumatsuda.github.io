DFA in GTP

Different researchers have claimed different aspects.

  • Proof writing -- Senk
  • Search control -- Schoenfeld
  • Concept understanding -- Moore

Can use those findings to build an ITS that overcomes students difficulties.

Interesting thesis

New idea is the best.

Old idea in a new context is good.  To do this, we need to fail to apply old idea in some context.

Giving an affirmative answer (i.e., YES) to a question that is intuitively true is not good.

Thesis on the goal reification or the feedback are interesting, in a sense that they are answering non-intuitive questions.

Method thesis

Implement a teaching method and claim how it works fine.

In our case:

Build an ITS,

Compare it to MTT,

Claim how our ITS works better than MTT.

In other words, we would claim a limitation of MTT (i.e., when MTT doesn't work).

Why teach construction?

Use construction problem to measure skill transfer.

It would be interesting if we can claim that "teaching construction at the early stage enhances students problem-solving skills."
