Need to distinguish a proposition mentioned by student as an "assertion" and a "presupposition."

Premise for AC-* hints and conclusion for AP-* hints must be a common ground, namely, the student and the tutor must agree with those propositions to make the hint sounds reasonable.

The ontology of GramyTutor

node vs. step (proposition vs. justification)

recently mentioned node vs. past node

trivial statement vs. non-trivial (e.g., "^ABC is an isosceles triangle" could be skipped)

agent of contribution (student vs. tutor)

GramyTutor provides a better hint on top of those ontology
