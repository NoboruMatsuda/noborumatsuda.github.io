Guided Problem-solving

It's equivalent to a model tracing tutor that provides immediate feedback to prevent students going down a garden path.  It is known that such learning system follows the principle of time-effect trade-off. 

Hence, we do not need to conduct any experiment on this issue. 

However, nobody known how the students reach mastery by different learning strategy (i.e., with or without having experience to go down garden path).
