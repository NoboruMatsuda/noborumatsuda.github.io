Experimental design

We can only measure accuracies in the paper-and-pencil condition

- Train all participants with the computer tutor.

- Provide posttest both on PC and paper-pencil.

Confounding in the item in order

When you see the desired speed up in the learning curve, would it be just a matter of students learning more and more easy postulates?

- Make sure that they are equally difficult,

- Shuffle the order of problems around

Power of the analysis
