Self explanation

Although AGT provides tutor's explanations, we do not compare it with student's self-explanation.  So, the claim in "Impact of proactive scaffolding" that says "we will see if learning is equally effective when students' self-explanation is replaced with tutor's self-explanation. 

 

Proactive Scaffolding

It's just providing instruction on problem solving.  Why use new term?

Scaffolding shouldn't just a direct instruction.  It must be something that help students to do their job.  Like scaffolding helps constructors to build up a building. 

The tutor does fade in and out scaffolding hence it is not just another type of instruction given.

The tutor does help students to learn cognitive skills through problem solving.  Solving problem is a mean to achieve mastery in specific cognitive skills, not a goal, hence the tutor does provide scaffolding.
