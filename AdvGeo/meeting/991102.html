Term project as building a prototype of AdvGeo tutor

  • Hooking up GRAMY to an ITS.
  • Goal scaffolding by reification.
  • Eliciting knowledge construction by meta cognitive prompting.

Design issues

  • To help students to construct auxiliary lines, by asking for an effective DS.
      Meta cognitive prompting: [Polya57]

      "Look at the conclusion.  And try to think of a familiar theorem having the same or a similar conclusion."
      "If two triangles are congruent, the corresponding angles are equal."
      "Good!  Now, here is a theorem related to yours and proved before.  Could you use it?"
      "I think so, but I do not see yet quite how."
      "Should you introduce some auxiliary element in order to make it use possible?"

      Reifying a DS application

      "Could you see the auxiliary element?"
      "....."
      "Here you can overlap this known theorem against the figure like this."
      "Yah, I could draw a perpendicular to make a triangle congruent to XYZ."

  • In general, to offer goal scaffolding as reifying a backward reasoning with DS
    • Highlight a goal.
    • Ask any DS which matches against the figure and has a conclusion corresponding to the goal.
    • Graphically show how the DS overlaps against the figure, so that the students can see "how it works."
  • And so for a forward reasoning with DS

Technical goals of the project

  • To implement a GUI that holds the properties described above.
  • To implement an interface between GRAMY and the GUI to provide the above functions.

Examples of the hinting strategies by Polya

  • What is the unknown?
  • Could you derive something useful from the data?
  • Do you know a related problem?
  • Is it possible to satisfy the condition?
  • Did you use all the data?
  • Have you seen before?

Reference

[Polya57] G. Polya, How to solve it, Doubleday Anchor Books, 1957.
